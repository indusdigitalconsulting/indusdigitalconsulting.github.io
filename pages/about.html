About Indus Digital Consulting

A Mississauga-based digital marketing agency passionate about helping local businesses thrive online.

Our Story

Built in the GTA, For the GTA

Indus Digital Consulting was founded with a simple mission: to help small and medium-sized businesses in Mississauga, Toronto, and across the Greater Toronto Area compete and win online.

We started as a small web design shop in Mississauga and have grown into a full-service digital marketing agency — but we've never lost our commitment to personal service, honest communication, and measurable results.

Over the years, we've worked with hundreds of businesses across dozens of industries — from law firms and restaurants to contractors and retail stores. We understand the unique challenges that GTA businesses face and we know what it takes to stand out in one of Canada's most competitive markets.
