The Google Business Profile Advantage

Your Google Business Profile (formerly Google My Business) is your most powerful local SEO asset. An optimised profile with accurate information, quality photos, regular posts, and strong reviews can put your business in the coveted "Local Pack" — the map results that appear above organic listings and capture the most clicks.
