Our Story

Helping GTA Businesses Win Online

Indus Digital Consulting was founded in Mississauga with one goal: to give local businesses the same digital firepower as the big players. Since day one, we've worked with hundreds of clients across the Greater Toronto Area — from neighbourhood service providers to growing mid-sized enterprises.
